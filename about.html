    BRAND STORY 品牌故事
    Conceptualized in 2017 by Justin Birmingham (JB), we are bringing traditional American BAR-B-QUE to Shanghai. The journey began when Justin toured Tennessee, Kentucky, Kansas City, and Texas, the Bar-B-Que meccas of the world, and the home of Kentucky Bourbon Whisky. 2017年，我们将美国传统BAR－B－Que引进了上海。最先提出这一想法的是Justin Birminghanm先生，当他参观了田纳西州、肯塔基州、堪萨斯城和德克萨斯州这些闻名全球的BBQ圣地以及肯塔基波本威士忌的故乡后，更加坚定了这一想法。
    In Memphis, Bar-B-Que become well-known due to the World Championship Bar-B-Que contest and has been listed in the Guinness World Records as the largest Bar-B-Que contest in the world. It was here that JB crafted and perfected his own classic Southern American style of dry rubs, smoking techniques, and unique Bar-B-Que recipes. 在孟菲斯，BBQ因世界锦标赛中的BBQ这一项目而闻名，并被列入了吉尼斯世界纪录，成为世界上最大型的BBQ赛事。JB先生正是在这里精益求精，完美地制作出属于他的经典南美洲工艺，包括干揉，烟熏技术和独特的BBQ秘制配方。
    The Bourbon Cookhouse has distilled decades of knowledge and experience into an irresistible selection of dishes. Everything on our menu is handled with tender loving care. Our imported USDA meats and seafood are dry rubbed and smoked over 24 hours. The BAR-B-QUE is slowly smoked to perfection with apple, cherry, hickory wood, then grilled over charcoal, finished with our secret HOUSE MOP sauce. The result are briskets, pork shoulders and ribs brimming with texture, a mouthwatering taste, and a juicy tenderness that you simply won’t forget. The Bourbon Cookhouse 将数十年的知识和经验浓缩成一份份极具诱惑的精品美食，将无微不至的关怀倾注在每一道菜品中。我们将USDA认证的进口优质肉品和海鲜干揉烟熏24小时，将BBQ使用果木、樱桃木及山核桃木慢慢的烟熏至完美，转而使用木炭继续烧烤，如此即制作完成了我们的秘制HOUSEMOP酱料。精心制作的酱料，令胸脯肉、猪肘肉和肋骨肉充满质感，鲜美多汁，口感极佳，令人垂涎欲滴，无法忘怀！
    There is no better companion to Bar-B-Que than barrel aged Bourbon, Hand Crafted Beer, and Fine Whiskey Cocktails. The Bourbon Cookhouse offers up more than 40 types of imported bourbons, barrel aged bourbon cocktails, and house infused bourbons creating what is best described as a Bourbon extravaganza. 享用BBQ美味的同时，是否还想畅饮一番？没有比桶装波本威士忌、手工酿造啤酒和上等波本威士忌鸡尾酒更好的选择了。The Bourbon Cookhouse 提供40多种进口波本威士忌及桶装波本鸡尾酒，诠释了波本的创造力，堪称是一场盛大无比的波本之宴！
    Come experience a cozy, rustic ambiance that will transport you to the American South. Escape to a world of great country music, unforgettable food and hand-crafted drinks at the Bourbon Cookhouse. 快来体验这个舒适纯朴的环境吧，我们将带您进入美国南部，聆听美妙的乡村音乐，享受令人难忘的美食和手工制作的饮料，感受The Bourbon Cookhouse带给您的美好！
    CHEF'S PROFILE 厨师简介
    Chef profile : About JUSTIN BIRMINGHAM 主厨简介 : 贾斯汀·伯明翰。

    Justin’s passion for good food began at a young age, joining a 2 Michelin Star restaurant in the UK at just 18 years old. Arriving in Shanghai in 2011, Justin went on to become the Group Executive Chef in over 8 infamous diversified authentic American, Indian, and Mexican restaurants throughout the city.
    He has spent years honing his preferred cooking styles, as well as his ambition to achieve brisket perfection; sometimes referred to as the ‘Holy Grail of ‘BBQ.’ This journey will continue and progress, while Justin pursues his passion for serving you the most authentic American Bar-B-Que in China.     Justin对美食的热爱始于年轻时代，18岁时在英国加入米其林二星餐厅。 Justin于2011年抵达上海，随后成为集团行政总厨，负责整个城市9个多元正宗化的美国，印度和墨西哥著名餐馆。他花费数年的时间磨练自己喜欢的烹饪风格，以及他追求完美的雄心壮志; 有时被称为'烧烤圣杯'，这一旅程将继续并取得进展，而贾斯汀追求他的热情，为您服务中国最真实的美国酒吧
