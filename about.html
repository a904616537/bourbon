    BRAND STORY 品牌故事
    The wait is finally over. Conceptualized in 2017 by Justin Birmingham (JB), we are bringing traditional American BAR-B-QUE to Shanghai. The journey began when Justin toured Tennessee, Kentucky, Kansas City, and Texas, the Bar-B-Que meccas of the world, and the home of Kentucky Bourbon Whisky. 经过了漫长的等待，2017年，我们将美国传统BAR－B－Que引进了上海。最先提出这一想法的是Justin Birmingham先生，当他参观了田纳西州、肯塔基州、堪萨斯城和德克萨斯州这些闻名全球的BBQ圣地以及肯塔基波本威士忌的故乡后，更加坚定了这一想法。
    In Memphis, Bar-B-Que become well-known due to the World Championship Bar-B-Que contest and has been listed in the Guinness World Records as the largest Bar-B-Que contest in the world. It was here that JB crafted and perfected his own classic Southern American style of dry rubs, smoking techniques, and unique Bar-B-Que recipes. 在孟菲斯，BBQ因世界锦标赛中的BBQ这一项目而闻名，并被列入了吉尼斯世界纪录，成为世界上最大型的BBQ赛事。JB先生正是在这里精益求精，完美地制作出属于他的经典南美洲工艺，包括干揉，烟熏技术和独特的BBQ秘制配方。
    Come experience a cozy, rustic ambiance that will transport you to the American South. Escape to a world of great country music, unforgettable food and hand-crafted drinks at the Bourbon Cookhouse. 快来体验这个舒适纯朴的环境吧，我们将带您进入美国南部，聆听美妙的乡村音乐，享受令人难忘的美食和手工制作的饮料，感受The Bourbon Cookhouse带给您的美好！
    CHEF'S PROFILE 厨师的形象
    Justin Justin

    Justin’s passion for good food began at a young age, joining a 2 Michelin Star restaurant in the UK at just 18 years of age. Arriving in Shanghai in 2011, Justin went on to become the Group Executive Chef in over 9 infamous diversified authentic American, Indian, and Mexican restaurants throughout the city.
    He has spent years honing his cooking style and desire for brisket perfection. This journey will continue and he’s looking forward to serving you the most authentic American Bar-B-Que in China.     Justin对美食的热爱始于年轻时代，18岁时在英国加入米其林二星餐厅。 Justin于2011年抵达上海，随后成为集团行政总厨，负责整个城市9个多元正宗化的美国，印度和墨西哥著名餐馆。他花了数年的时间磨练自己的烹饪风格并渴望完美。 这次旅程将继续下去，他期待着在中国为您提供最正宗的美国特色烤肉
