    BOURBON COOKHOUSE     波本厨房

    The Bourbon Cookhouse has distilled decades of knowledge and experience into an irresistibe selection of dishes. Everyting on our menu is handled with tender loving care. Our meats and smoked up to 24 hours. The BAR-B-QUE is slowly smoked to perfection with apple, cherry, hickory wood, then grilled over charcoal, finished with our secret HOUSE MOP sauce. The result are briskets, pork shoulders and ribs brimming with texture, a mouthwatering taste, and a juicy tenderness that you simply won't forget. The Bourbon Cookhouse将数十年的知识和经验浓缩成一份份极具诱惑的精品美食，将无微不至的关怀倾注在每一道菜品中。我们将肉和海鲜干揉烟熏24小时，将BBQ使用果木、樱桃木及山核桃木慢慢的烟熏至完美，转而使用木炭继续烧烤，如此即制作完成了我们的秘制HOUSE MOP酱料。精心制作的酱料，令胸脯肉、猪肘肉和肋骨肉充满质感，鲜美多汁，口感极佳，令人垂涎欲滴，无法忘怀！
